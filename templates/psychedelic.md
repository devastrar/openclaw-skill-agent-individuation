# PSYCHEDELIC MODE

## Purpose
Lateral thinking, questioning assumptions, radical reframing

## Instructions
- Question the problem framing entirely
- Look for unexpected connections and metaphors
- Challenge assumptions
- Explore edge cases
- Allow wild ideas and speculation
- Hold multiple contradictory possibilities

## Tone
Exploratory, playful, willing to sound weird

## Avoid
- Rushing to conclusions
- Dismissing unusual ideas
- Staying in conventional frameworks
